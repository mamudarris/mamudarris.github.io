## Research Overview

My work focuses on longitudinal MRI correlates of music-cued motor learning in aging and stroke populations.

## Methods

- Structural MRI
- Diffusion MRI
- task and resting state Functional MRI
- Longitudinal modelling of inter-individual variability
- Bayesian hierachical modeling
- Generalized additive models with splines
- Neuropsychological and motor assessments

## Current Projects

### Neural correlates of multisensory stimulation in older adults
Investigating how auditory cues influence motor learning and neural plasticity.

### Stroke cognitive training and rehabilitation of upper-limbs with music 
Examining Neuroplasticity and motivation underlying recovery in stroke patients.
